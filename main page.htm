Departments

Telecardiology

    Telecardiology is a modern medical practice, 
  which uses the power of telecommunications to 
  achieve remote diagnosis and treatment of 
  heart disease. It includes coronary heart
  disease, arrhythmias, congestive cardiac 
  failure and sudden cardiac arrest.

Teleradiology

   Teleradiology is the transmission of the
 radiological patient images, such as x-rays, CTs, 
 and MRIs, from one location to another for the 
 purposes of   sharing studies with other 
 radiologists and physicians.

Teledentistry

    Teledentistry refers to the use of telehealth 
  systems and methodologies in dentistry. 
  Telehealth refers to a broad variety of 
  technologies and   tactics to deliver virtual 
  medical, health, and education services.
